Projects

Renewal of Non-Recurring Fee Payment Process (2023)

As one of the HR representatives, I led the planning, testing, and implementation of a new electronic process for handling non-recurring fee payments. The initiative streamlined the workflow and enhanced user experience. As part of the project, two new system integrations were developed: one linking the fee system to the payroll system, and another connecting it to an electronic archive system.

The updated process was piloted in 2023 and successfully launched organization-wide in 2024, replacing manual procedures with a more efficient and transparent digital solution. Since the launch, I have acted as both the HR system administrator and the process owner.
